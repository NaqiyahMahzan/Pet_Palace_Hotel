FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions)

General FAQs

Q1: How can I adopt a pet from Pet Palace?

A: To adopt a pet, visit our adoption center, fill out an application, and go through our screening process.

Q2: What is the adoption fee used for?

A: The adoption fee covers vaccinations, spaying/neutering, and general care to ensure the health and well-being of our pets.

Q3: Can I adopt if I live in an apartment?

A: Yes, you can adopt a pet even if you live in an apartment. However, we will assess the suitability of the living conditions during the screening process.

Pet Care FAQs

Q1: What should I feed my adopted pet?

A: Our staff will provide you with detailed information about your pet's diet and nutritional needs at the time of adoption.

Q2: Do you provide veterinary care after adoption?

A: Yes, all adopted pets receive initial veterinary care, and we can guide you on further veterinary care requirements.

Q3: Can I return the pet if I encounter difficulties?

A: Yes, you can return the pet to Pet Palace if you face challenges in caring for it. We are here to support you and the well-being of the pet.
