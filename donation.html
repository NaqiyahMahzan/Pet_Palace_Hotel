Uteh, a stray cat we discovered approximately 100 meters from our pet hotel, has unfortunately lost her right ear.

Though we've taken her to the doctor, the full extent of Uteh's condition is still under assessment.

Following a blood test conducted on December 29, 2023, it was determined that Uteh is positive for FIV (Feline Immunodeficiency Virus).

This condition compromises the immune system of cats, making them susceptible to various infections.

Additionally, the blood test revealed that Uteh is also dealing with jaundice, low blood pressure, and anemia.

We are closely monitoring Uteh's health and working with the veterinarian to provide the necessary care and support for her well-being.

Thank you for your donation to help treat Uteh's health issues.

Your support means a lot and will go towards getting her the care she needs.

We're grateful for your kindness!
