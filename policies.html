POLICY

Room Reservation Policy

Booking Process:

  • Room reservations can be made online or in-person at our facility.
  • Reservations are subject to availability.

    Cancellation:

  • A cancellation fee may apply if a reservation is canceled within a specified period.

    Check-In and Check-Out:

  • Check-in and check-out times for rooms will be communicated during the reservation process.

    Pet Requirements:

  • Pets staying in reserved rooms must meet vaccination and health requirements.

    Pet Adoption Policy

    Adoption Eligibility:

  • Prospective adopters must be at least 18 years old. A thorough screening process will be conducted to ensure a suitable living environment for the pet.

    Home Visit:

  • A representative from Pet Palace may conduct a home visit to assess the living conditions for the adopted pet.

    Vaccination and Neutering:

  • All adopted pets will be vaccinated and, if age-appropriate, neutered before adoption.

    Adoption Fee:

  • An adoption fee is required to cover the cost of vaccinations, spaying/neutering, and general care.

    Return Policy:

  • If an adopter is unable to keep the pet, they must return it to Pet Palace rather than rehoming it independently.

    Follow-Up Checks:

  • Pet Palace reserves the right to conduct follow-up checks to ensure the well-being of the adopted pets.

    Grooming Services Policy

    Appointment:

  • Grooming services are available by appointment. Walk-ins may be accommodated based on availability.

    Pet Health Check:

  • Pets must be in good health to undergo grooming. Any health concerns should be communicated during the appointment booking.

    Pick-Up Time:

  • Pet owners will be notified when grooming is complete, and pets should be picked up promptly.

    Veterinary Services Policy

    Appointment and Emergency Care:

  • Veterinary services are available by appointment. Emergency cases will be accommodated based on severity.

    Medical Records:

  • Pet owners are required to provide relevant medical records for accurate veterinary care.

    Treatment Plans:

  • Veterinarians will communicate treatment plans and costs before proceeding with any medical procedures.

    Follow-Up Care:

  • Pet owners should follow any post-treatment care instructions provided by the veterinarian.
